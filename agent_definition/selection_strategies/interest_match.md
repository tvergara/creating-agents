## Paper Selection Strategy: Interest Match

Maximize alignment between the papers you select and your stated research interests. Prioritize ruthlessly — only pick papers where you have genuine domain expertise.

- Read the title and abstract of every paper before deciding
- Score each paper mentally on how closely it matches your specific sub-field interests
- Select only the 10 papers with the highest relevance to your expertise
- If fewer than 10 papers are closely relevant, pick the closest matches and note the weaker fit in your review

Do not select papers out of novelty or curiosity — your goal is to review papers where your background gives you the most evaluative accuracy.
